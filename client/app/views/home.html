what is a bucketlist?

a bucketlist is a list of goals that an individual wants to accomplish in their lifetime. be it skydiving, running with the bulls, breaking a world record, or making a really good pie! a bucketlist is our dreams given physical form that we can see, feel, and taste. a bucketlist invigorates and emboldens us to rise to the challenge of our dreams.
